Background Info About Gorilla Typing Project

GorillaType is a minimalistic and customizable typing test. It features many test modes GorillaType attempts to emulate the experience of natural keyboard typing during a typing test,by unobtrusively presenting the text prompts and displaying typed characters in-placeproviding straightforward, feedback on typos, speed, and accuracy.Test yourself in various modes, and improve your speed.
